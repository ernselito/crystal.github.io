Case Study Details

Foundational & Evaluative Research for the Republic Bank Mobile App Launch.

UX Research
UX Research Associate
Republic Bank
User Interviews Surveys Usability Testing Prototype Testing Thematic Analysis

Republic Bank Mobile App

Goal: Design for 50,000+ users

Before launching its first mobile app, Republic Bank wanted to ensure the platform met customer expectations and digital literacy levels. This project strengthened my understanding of evaluative research and how user feedback drives product readiness.

Research Questions

To ensure accessibility and trust, we focused on answering two primary questions:

  • What features do users value most in mobile banking?
  • How intuitive is the navigation for first-time users?

The Approach

We conducted 10 in-depth interviews and 20 usability tests with recruited pilot users. Through this process, we identified major usability gaps, specifically regarding navigation confusion and login friction. I worked directly with IT to iterate the design based on this feedback.

Findings & Insights

70% of users struggled to locate key functions on the home screen during initial testing. The insights revealed that simplifying the menu significantly improved satisfaction.

Impact & Results

  • 40% Reduction in task completion time after menu simplification.
  • Smoother Onboarding and higher user confidence reported.
  • Findings directly informed final design changes before rollout.
  • Design validated for 50,000+ users.
