Online Retirement Platform Enhancement

Goal: Improve usability & engagement

This project reinforced the value of continuous evaluation and data-informed iteration in user-centered design. By synthesizing feedback from 401(k) participants, we were able to drive significant improvements in digital adoption.

The Goal

The primary objective was to improve the usability and engagement of Vanguard’s online retirement platform. We needed to identify why users weren't fully utilizing digital tools and where they encountered friction.

Process & Analysis

We conducted post-launch surveys and A/B testing to evaluate digital tool enhancements. By synthesizing qualitative and quantitative feedback from 401(k) participants, we successfully identified high-friction points in critical transaction flows.

Collaboration

A key part of the success was cross-functional collaboration. I worked directly with product managers and designers to interpret the data and prioritize usability fixes that would have the highest impact on user experience.

Impact & Results

  • 30% Improvement in adoption of new tools.
  • 15% Reduction in digital errors during transactions.
  • Successfully removed high-friction points in transaction flows.
  • Established a framework for ongoing iterative testing.
